Ant traps


Pest attracted
Ants

Materials required
Plastic pan or aluminum pan of 1 ft. square
Glue/sticky material

Methodology
Make a hole in pan (plastic or aluminum) about one foot length and six inches width. Push pan into the sloping downwards. Coat the bottom of the pan with sticky materials. Check the pans regularly to make sure that the baits are sticky enough to trap the ants.

Benefits
Barriers prevent ants from attacking the sown seeds and seedlings that are grown in free standing seedbeds
