Mandarin cultivation in Arunachal Pradesh


Khasi Mandarin is an important fruit crop cultivated in the state of Arunachal Pradesh, particularly in East Siang  and its sorounding districts. College of Horticulture and Forestry (CHF), Central Agricultural University, Pasighat has developed various technologies for scientific production and management of Khasi Mandarin.
