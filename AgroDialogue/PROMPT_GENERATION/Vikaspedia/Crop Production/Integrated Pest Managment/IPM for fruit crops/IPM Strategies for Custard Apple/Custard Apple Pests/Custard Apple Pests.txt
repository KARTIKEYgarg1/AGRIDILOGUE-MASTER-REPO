Custard Apple Pests


Pests of national significance

Insect and mite pests


Mealy bug: Ferrisia virgata Cockerell (Hemiptera: Pseudococcidae) 
Fruit fly: Bactrocera spp, B. zonata Saunders (Diptera: Tephritidae) 
Scales: Ceroplastes floridensis Comstock (Hemiptera: Coccidae) 
Fruit boring caterpillar: Heterographis bengalella Ragonot (Syn: Anonaepestis      bengalella) (Lepidoptera: Pyralidae) 

Diseases


Fruit rot &amp; Anthracnose: Colletotrichum gloeosporioides (Penz.) Penz. and Sacc. (Perfect stage Glomerella cingulata (Stoneman) Spauld. &amp; H. Schrenk 
 Alternaria leaf spot: Alternaria spp. 
 Cylindrocladium leaf spot: Cylindrocladium colhounii and Cylindrocladium scoparium 
 Diplodia rot: Botryodiplodia theobromae Patouillard 
Black canker: Phomopsis annonacearum (Bond) Mont. 

Nematodes


Spiral nematode: Helicotylenchus spp. 
Stint nematode: Tylenchorhynchus spp.
