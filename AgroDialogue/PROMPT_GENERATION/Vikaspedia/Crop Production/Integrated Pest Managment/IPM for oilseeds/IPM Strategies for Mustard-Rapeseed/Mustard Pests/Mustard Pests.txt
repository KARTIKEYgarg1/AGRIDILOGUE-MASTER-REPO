Mustard Pests


Pests of National Significance

Insect pests


Mustard aphid: Lipaphis erysimi (Kaltenbach) (Hemiptera: Aphididae)
Painted bug: Bagrada hilaris (Burmeister) (Hemiptera: Pentatomidae)
Mustard saw fly: Athalia lugens proxima (Klug) (Hymenoptera: Tenthredinidae)
Mustard leaf miner: Chromatomyia horticola (Goureau) (Diptera: Agromyzidae)

Diseases


Alternaria blight: Alternaria brassicae (Berk.) Sacc, A. brassicicola (Schwein. Wiltshire)
White rust: Albugo candida (Pers.) Kuntze
Powdery Mildew: Erysiphe cruciferum Opiz ex L. Junnell

Weeds

Broad leaf weeds

Lambs quarter: Chenopodium album L. (Chenopodiaceae)
Scarlet pimpernel: Anagallis arvensis L. (Primulaceae)
Sweet clover: Melilotus indica (L.) All. (Fabaceae)
Fine leaf fumitory: Fumaria parvifl ora Lam. (Fumariaceae)
Corn spurry: Spergula arvensis L. (Caryophyllaceae)
Field bindweed: Convolvulus arvensis L. (Convolvulaceae)
Wild Onion: Asphodelus tenuifolius Cav. (Liliaceae)
Burcloveru: Medicago denticulata Willd. (Fabaceae)
Common vetch: Vicia sativa L. (Fabaceae)
Yellow pea: Lathyrus aphaca L. (Fabaceae)

Grassy weeds

Bluegrass: Poa annua L. (Poaceae)
Canary Grass: Phalaris minor Retz. (Poaceae)

Sedges

Purple nutsedge: Cyperus rotundus L. (Cyperaceae)

Parasitic weed

Broomrape: Orobanche aegyptiaca L. (Orobanchaceae)

Pests of Regional Significance

Insect pests


Bihar hairy caterpillar: Spilosoma obliqua Walker (Lepidoptera : Arctiidae)
Cabbage head borer: Hellula undalis Fabricius (Lepidoptera: Crambidae)
Diamondback moth: Plutella xylostella L. (Lepidoptera: Plutellidae)
Larger moth (leaf webber): Crocidolomia binotalis Fabricius (Lepidoptera: Pyralidae)

Diseases


Sclerotinia stem rot: Sclerotinia sclerotiorum (Lib.) de Bary
Downy mildew: Hyaloperonospora parasitica Pers.
Club root: Plasmodiophora brassicae Woronin
Bacterial Leaf spot/ blight: Xanthomonas campestris pv. campestris Pammel (Dowson)
