Kisan Sarathi digital platform

Digital Platform 'Kisan Sarathi' has been launched to facilitate farmers to get ‘right information at right time’ in their desired language.



About the platform

Kisan Sarathi is an Intelligent Online Agricultural Technology Platform for supporting agriculture at a local niche with a national perspective. The Kisan Sarathi provides the facility for each level of monitoring and reporting through an interactive dashboard ranging from the , Institutes to the Headquarters. The platform enables the officials at the various levels to view and monitor the daily activities like farmers’ registration, live calls, total calls, messages pushed, given advisories and pending advisories, etc.
