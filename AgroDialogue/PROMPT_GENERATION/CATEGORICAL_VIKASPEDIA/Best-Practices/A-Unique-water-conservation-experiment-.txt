A Unique water conservation experiment 

About the experiment
Water is a very important element in the agricultural scenario. Water conservation is indeed a need of the hour and this experiment showcases the technique to conserve water for optimum agricultural usage, resulting in maximization of the farming returns to the 'Farmer'. The experiment done on the foundation of 'Rain water harvesting' including 'Drip Irrigation' and 'Rain Irrigation' techniques.
The experiment has been documented as part of the Mandi.com TV show of National Commodity & Derivatives Exchange Limited (NCDEX) telecasted in DD Kisan. The show is telecasted on every Sunday at 8 am with a repeat telecast at 4.30 pm and 11 pm.

Location of the experiment
The amazing experiment was done by ''Dindori Ashram" in Nashik, Maharashtra.
