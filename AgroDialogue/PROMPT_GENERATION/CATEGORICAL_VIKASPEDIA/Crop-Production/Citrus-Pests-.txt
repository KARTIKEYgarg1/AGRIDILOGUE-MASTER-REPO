Citrus Pests 


Pests of National Significance

Insect pests


Aphid: Toxoptera aurantii (Boyer de Fonscolombe) (Homoptera: Aphididae)
Citrus psylla: Diaphorina citri (Kuwayama) (Hemiptera: Psyllidae)
Citrus leaf miner: Phyllocnistis citrella (Stainton) (Lepidoptera: Gracillariidae)
Mealybug : Planococcus citri (Risso) (Hemiptera: Pseudococcidae)
Fruit sucking moth: Eudocima fullonica, E. materna (Clerck) (Lepidoptera: Noctuidae)
Citrus/Lemon butterfly: Papilio demoleus, P. polytes (Linnaeus) (Lepidoptera: Papilionidae)

Diseases


Gummosis: Phytophthora spp.
Fruit rot: Phytophthora palmivora (Butler)
Anthracnose: Colletotrichum gloeosporioides
Scab: Elsinoe fawcettii (Bitanc &amp; Jenkins)
Sooty mould: Capnodium citri (Berk and Desm), Meliola butleri (Briosi and Pass)
Citrus canker: Xanthomonas axonopodis pv. citri (Hasse)
Tristeza: Citrus Tristeza Virus
Greening: Candidatus liberibacter (Jagoueix)

Nematodes


Citrus nematode: Tylenchulus semipenetrans (Cobb) (Tylenchida: Tylenchulidae)
Lance nematode: Hoplolaimus indicus (Sher) (Tylenchida: Haplolaimidae)
Lesion nematode: Pratylenchus coff ee (Goodey) (Tylenchida: Pratyleanchidae)

Weeds

Broad leaf weeds

Tropical spiderwort: Commelina benghalensis L. (Commelinaceae)
Swine cress: Coronopus didymus (L.) Sm. (Brassicaceae)
Horse purslane: Trianthema portulacastrum L. (Aizoaceae)
Black nightshade: Solanum nigrum L. (Solanaceae)
False amaranth: Digera arvensis Forssk. (Amaranthaceae)
Puncture vine:  L. (Zygophyllaceae)
Field bindweed: Convolvulus arvensis L. (Convolvulaceae)
Common cocklebur: Xanthium strumarium L. (Asteraceae)
Spurge: Euphorbia hirta L. (Euphorbiaceae)
Carrot grass: Parthenium hysterophorus L. (Asteraceae)

Grassy weeds

Bermuda grass: Cynodon dactylon (L.) Pers., (Poaceae)
Annual brachiaria: Brachiaria defl exa (Schumach.) Robyns (Poaceae)
Viper grass: Dinebra retrofl exa (Vahl.) Panzer. (Poaceae)

Sedges

Purple nut sedge: Cyperus rotundus L., (Cyperaceae)
Flat sedge: Cyperus iria L. (Cyperaceae)

Pests of Regional Significance

Insect and mite pests


Citrus blackfly : Aleurocanthus woglumi (Ashby) (Hemiptera: Aleurocanthus) (Punjab, Maharashtra, Karnataka,  and )
Root grub: Holotrichia spp. (Coleoptera: Scarabaeidae)
Soft scale: Coccus hesperidium (Handlirsch) (Hemiptera: Coccoidea) (South India).
Armoured scale: Aonidiella aurantii (Maskell) (Hemiptera: Diaspididae) (Meghalaya, Punjab, , Maharashtra,West Bengal and Karnataka)
Stem borer: Chloridolum alcmene (Guenee) (Lepidoptera: Noctuidae) ()
Mites: Eutetranychus orientalis (Klein) (Prostigmata: Tetranychidae) (Meghalaya, Punjab, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Haryana, Karnataka, Delhi, Jammu &amp; Kashmir)
Thrips: Scirtothrips citri (Shull) (Thysanoptera: Thripidae) (Jammu &amp; Kashmir)

Diseases


Powdery mildew: Oidium citri (Link) (Uttar Pradesh)
Sapling wilt: Fusarium solani (W.C. Snyder &amp; H. N. Hansen)
Blue mould rot: Penicillium italicum (Wehmer)(Tamil Nadu, Haryana, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh)
Green mould rot: Penicillium digitatum (Pers &amp; Sacc) (Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Uttar Pradesh)
Sour rot: Geotrichium candidum (Link) (Maharashtra,  and Madhya Pradesh)
