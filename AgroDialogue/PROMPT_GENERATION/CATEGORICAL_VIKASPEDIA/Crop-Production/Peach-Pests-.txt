Insect pest


Stem borer: Aeolesthes sarta Solsky (Coleoptera: Cerambycidae) 
Flat headed borers: Chrysobothris mali Eschscholtz (Coleoptera: Buprestide) &amp;Capnodis tenebrionis Linne (Coleoptera: Buprestidae)

Defoliating beetle: Protactia neglecta &amp; Adoretus sp. (Coleoptera: Scarabaeidae)

Apricot brown scale: Lecanium corni (Bouché) (Hemiptera: Coccidae) 
Peach leaf curl aphid: Brachycaudus helichrysi Kaltenbach (Hemiptera: Aphididae)
