Human resource development 

Human resource is one of the most important resources of our country. Providing training, exposure tour to staff of the department and progressive farmers will help to develop their skills and enhance efficiency and productivity.

Farmers tour to mainland (agatti, amini, andrott, kalpeni, kavaratti& minicoy)

The farmers are taken to mainland for exposure visit / tour to research stations, universities etc to get the first hand information on the latest technology development in the different field of agriculture and allied sectors.

Awards / recognition (all islands)

The awards are given to the outstanding farmer, best coconut climber etc.
