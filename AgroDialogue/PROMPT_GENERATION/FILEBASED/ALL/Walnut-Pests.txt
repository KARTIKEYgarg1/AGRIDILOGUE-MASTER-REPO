Walnut pests

Pests of national significance

Insect pests

Stem borer: aeolesthes sarta solsky (coleoptera: cerambycidae) 
Bark beetle/shot-hole borer: scolytus nitidus schedl (coleoptera: scolytidae)
Gypsy moth: lymantria obfuscata walker (lepidoptera: lymantriidae) 
Leaf roller: archips argyrospilus (walker) (lepidoptera: tortricidae) 
Mealybug: drosicha dalbergiae (stebbing) (hemiptera: margarodidae) 
Grey weevil: myllocerus sp. (coleoptera: curculionidae) 
Walnut weevil: alcides porrectirostris marshall (coleoptera: curculionidae) 

Diseases

Leaf spot: alternaria sp 
Leaf blotch: marsonina juglanse (lib.) magnus and gnomonia leptostyla (fr.) ces. &amp; de not.
Dieback: glomerella cingulata (stoneman) spauld. &amp; h. Schrenk 
Powdery mildew: phyllactinia spp. 
Walnut blight: xanthomonas campestris (pammel 1895) dowson 
Anthracnose: gnomonia leptostyla (fr.) ces. &amp; de not., 
Phytophthora root and crown rot: phytophthora spp 
Armillaria root rot (oak root fungus): armillaria mellea (vahl) p.kumm. 
Crown gall: agrobacterium tumefaciens smith &amp; townsend, 
Walnut canker: geosmithia morbida m.kolařík, e.freeland, c.utley &amp; tisserat 

Weeds

Broadleaf

Tropical spiderwort: commelina benghalensis l. (commelinaceae) 
Creeping wood sorrel: oxalis corniculata l. (oxalidaceae) 
Goat weed: ageratum conyzoides l. (asteraceae) 
Sowthistles: sonchus spp. (asteraceae) 
Congress grass: parthenium hysterophorus l. (asteraceae) 
Lambs quarter: chenopodium album l. (chenopodiaceae) 
Sweet yellow clover: melilotus indica (l.) all. (fabaceae) 
Toothed dock: rumex dentatus l. (polygonaceae) grasses 
Bermuda grass: cynodon dactylon (poaceae) 
Cogon grass: imperata cylindrica (l.) raeusch. (poaceae) 
Blanket grass: axonopus compressus (sw.) beauv. (poaceae) 
Large crabgrass: digitaria sanguinalis l. (scop.) (poaceae) 
Knot grass: paspalum distichum l. (poaceae) sedges 
Purple nutsedge: cyperus rotundus l. (cypraceae)

Pests of regional significance

Insect pests

Aphids: chromaphis juglandicola (kaltenbach) &amp; panaphis juglandis (goeze) (hemiptera: aphididae) 
Coding moth: cydia pomonella linnaeus (lepidoptera: lasiocampidae) 
San jose scale: quadraspidiotus perniciosus (comstock) (hemiptera: diaspididae)
