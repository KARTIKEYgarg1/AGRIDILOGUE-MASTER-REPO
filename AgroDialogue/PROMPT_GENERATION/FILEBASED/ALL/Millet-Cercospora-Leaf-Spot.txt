Millet Cercospora Leaf Spot

Symptoms
Small, oval lesions with grey centers and dark margins appear on leaves and stem. On these lesions black and raised dots develop.

Product Recommendations
Organic Control
There is no alternative treatment for this disease. Implement preventive measures to reduce the risk of infection in subsequent growing seasons.
Chemical Control
No chemical treatment is needed for this disease. Implement preventive measures to reduce the risk of infection in subsequent growing seasons.

What caused it?
High temperatures and high humidity favor the disease. The fungus is spread by wind and rain. It survives in crop residues and on alternative hosts like weeds. Yield losses are mostly small.

Preventive Measures 
Plant more resilient varieties.
Control weeds.
Implement crop rotation.
Maintain good field hygiene - remove or burn any plant residues.
