Arecanut pests

Pests of national significance

 insect and mite pests

 spindle bug: carvalhoia/ arecae miller &amp; china (hemiptera: miridae)
 inflorescence caterpillar: tirathaba mundella walker (lepidoptera: pyralidae)
 pentatomid bug: halyomorpha marmorea (fab.) (hemiptera: pentatomidae)
 root grub: leucopholis burmeisteri brenske), leucopholis lepidophora blanch (coleoptera: melolonthidae)
 white mite/sorghum mite: oligonychus indicus hirst (acarina: tetranychidae)
 red mite/palm mite: raoiella indica hirst (acarina: tenuipalpidae)

 diseases

 koleroga or mahali or fruit rot: phytophthora meadii mcrae
 inflorescence die back: colletotrichum gloeosporioides penz.
 anabe roga or basal stem rot or foot rot: ganoderma lucidum (leys) karst 
 bud rot or crown rot: phytophthora meadii mcrae 
 yellow leaf disease: phytoplasma disease transmitted by the plant hopper, proutista moesta (hemiptera: derbidae) 

 nematode 

 burrowing nematode: radopholus similis (cobb) thorne

 rodents 

 lesser bandicoot: bandicota bengalensis (gray) (throughout india) 
 palm rat/house rat: rattus rattus (linnaeus) (throughout india)

 goose grass: eleusine indica (l.) gaertner. (poaceae) 

Broadleaf

 pigweed: amaranthus viridis hook. F. (amaranthaceae)

 coat buttons: tridax procumbens l. (fabaceae)

 mile a minute: mickania micrantha h.b.k. (asteraceae)

 sedges

 purple nutsedge: cyperus rotundus l. (cyperaceae) 
 flat sedge: cyperus iria l. (cyperaceae)

 insect pests

 arecanut beetle: caccotrypes carpophagus horn (coleoptera: scolytidae)

Cigarette beetle: lasioderma serricorne (f.) (coleoptera: anobiidae)
 rice moth: corcyra cephalonica (stainton) (lepidoptera: galleriidae)
