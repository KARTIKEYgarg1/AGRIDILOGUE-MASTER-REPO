White board traps for snails

Materials required
Wooden boards
White paint, brush
Bucket
Detergent
Attractant like potato , cabbage

Methodology
Take white surface wooden boards (30 cm x 30 cm size). Place them along the plant rows. At daytime, snails take shelter underneath the cooler places. Check the traps before sunset and collect the snails. Put these traps in a bucket of soapy water or kill them.
