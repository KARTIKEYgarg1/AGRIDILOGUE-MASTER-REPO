Small cardmom: pests

Pests of national significance

Insect pests

Cardamom thrips: sciothrips cardamomi ramakrishna (thysanoptera: thripidae)
Shoot/panicle/capsule borer: conogethes punctiferalis guenée (lepidoptera: pyralidae)
Early capsule borer: jamides alecto felder (lepidoptera: lycaenidae)
Rootgrub: basilepta fulvicornis jacoby (coleoptera: chrysomelidae)

Diseases

‘katte’ or mosaic or marble disease: cardamom mosaic virus
Primary nursery leaf spot: phyllosticta elettariae chowdhury
Capsule rot/azhukal disease: phytophthora nicotianae var. Nicotianae breda de haan, p. Meadii mcrae
Nursery leaf rot: fusarium spp., alternaria spp.
Damping off or seedling rot: pythium vexans de bary and rhizoctonia solani j.g. Kühn, fusarium oxysporum schlecht
Clump rot or rhizome rot: pythium vexans de bary, rhizoctonia solani kühn and fusarium sp.

Nematode

Root-knot nematode: meloidogyne spp.

Weeds

Broadleaf

Goat weed: ageratum conyzoides l. (asteraceae)
Spanish needles: bidens pilosa l. (asteraceae)
Creeping wood sorrel: oxalis corniculata l. (oxalidaceae)
Neanotis wightiana wallich ex wight &amp; arnott) n. Indica (rubiaceae)
Soft blumea: blumea wightiana d. (asteraceae)
Little ironweed: vernonia cineria (l.) less. (asteraceae)
Asiatic pennywort: l. (apiaceae)

Grasses

Bermuda grass: cynodon dactylon (l) (poaceae)
Crowfoot grass: dactyloctenium aegyptium (l.) willd. (poaceae)
Wrinkled duck-beak: ischaemum rugosum salisb. (poaceae)
Buffalo grass: paspalum conjugatum bergius (poaceae)
Bunch grass: eragrostis tanella (l.) roem.&amp; schult. (poaceae)
Wavy leaf basket grass: oplimenus undulatifolius (ard.) (poaceae)

Sedges

Purple nutsedge: cyperus rotundus l. (cyperaceae)

Rodents

Three stripped squirrel: funambulus palmarum (linnaeus)
Lesser bandicoot: bandicota bengalensis (gray)

Pests of regional significance

Insect and mite pests

Cardamom whitefly: kanakarajiella cardamomi david and subramaniam (hemiptera: aleyrodidae)
Hairy caterpillars: eupterote undata, e. Fabia, e. Cardamomi, e. Canaraica, e. Mollis and e. Blanda (lepidoptera: eupterotidae)
Shoot fly: formosina flavipes, malloch (diptera: chlopidae)
Lacewing bug: stephanitis typicus distant (hemiptera: tingidae)
