Sesame pests

Pests of national significance

Insect pests

Leaf webber or roller and capsule borer: antigastra catalaunalis duponchel (lepidoptera: crambidae)
Gall fly: asphondylia sesame felt (diptera: cecidomyiidae)
Leaf hopper: orosius albicinctus distant (hemiptera: cicadellidae)

Diseases

Phyllody: phytoplasma like organism
Dry root rot: rhizoctonia bataticola taubenh
Phytophthora blight: phytophthora parasitica var. Sesame dastur
Alternaria blight: alternaria sesame kawamura (mohanty &amp; behera)

Weeds

Broadleaf

Pigweed: amaranthus viridis hook. F. (amaranthaceae)
Swine cress: coronopus didymus(l.) sm. (brassicaceae)
Black nightshade: solanum nigrum l. (solanaceae)
False amaranth: digera arvensis forssk. (amaranthaceae)
Horse purslane: trianthema portulacastrum l. (aizoaceae)
Cock’s comb: celosia argentea l. (amaranthaceae)
Common purselane: portulaca oleracea l. (portulacaceae)
Asthma herb: euphorbia hirta l. (euphorbiaceae)

Grasses

Goose grass: eleusine indica (l.) gaertner (poaceae)
Rabbit/crow foot grass: dactyloctenium aegyptium (l.) willd (poaceae)
Crab grass: digiteria sanguinalis (l.) scop. (poaceae)
Burmuda grass: cynodon dactylon (l.) pers. (poaceae)

Sedges

Purplenut sedge: cyperus rotundus l. (cyperaceae)
Flat sedge: cyperus iria l. (cyperaceae)

Pest of regional significance

Insect pests

Sphinx moth or hawk moth: acherontia styx westwood (lepidoptera: sphingidae)
Bihar hairy caterpillar: spilosoma obliqua walker (lepidoptera: arctiidae) (, , bihar, delhi, punjab)
Red hairy caterpillar: amsacta albistriga walker (lepidoptera: arctiidae) (rajasthan)
Pod bug: elasmolomus sordidus fabricius (hemiptera: lygaeidae)
Pentatomid bug: nezara viridula (linnaeus) (hemiptera: pentatomidae)
Cotton aphid: aphis gossypii glover (hemiptera: aphididae)

Diseases

Wilt: fusarium oxysporum schlecht. F. Sp. Sesame (maharashtra, rajasthan)
Bacterial blight: xanthomonas campestris dowson pv. Sesame (assam, uttar pradesh, , delhi)
Powdery mildew: sphaerotheca fuliginea (schltdl.) pollacci, leveillula taurica(lév.) g. Arnaud, erysiphe cichoracearum (dc) (rajasthan)

Nematode

 cyst nematode: heterodera cajani koshy (tylenchida: heteroderidae)
